Resources for Child Safety

Steps Taken by the Government

  • POCSO Act (2012): Protects children from sexual offenses.
  • Childline 1098: 24x7 helpline for children in distress.
  • Operation Smile: Rescues missing and trafficked children.
  • Beti Bachao Beti Padhao: Supports girl safety and education.
  • School Safety Guidelines: Enforces safe school environments.

Whom to Contact If You Feel Unsafe

  • Parents: Always share your feelings and problems with them.
  • Teachers or School Counselor: Trusted adults at school are there to help.
  • Police: Dial 100 or visit the nearest station.
  • Childline: Dial 1098 ‚Äî free, confidential, and 24x7.
  • Women & Child Welfare Dept: Visit your local office for legal support and protection.
